Who is this website for?

It's for YOU, Top Gear fan!

I've been watching Top Gear since I was a teenager, and have always found it frustrating and time-consuming to find that one episode I'm thinking of. Whether it be a specific car review, special guest, or cheap car challenge, there's no shortage of episodes to remember!

Frustrated with existing search options, in late 2016 I took it upon myself to catalog and organize each and every series of Top Gear (with a lot of help from Wikipedia) into a searchable database.

Top Gear Search is designed to be simple, easy-to-use, and lightweight. Using simple keywords, relevant episodes with full, detailed descriptions are returned to the user.

This is a continuing personal project of mine, so it will continuously recieve updates and enhancements. If you think of a feature you'd like to see, or if you encounter a bug/missing data, please don't hesitate to contact me at contact@bpwalters.com.

Thanks again for stopping by! I hope this site is as helpful for you as it's been for me.

-Ben
